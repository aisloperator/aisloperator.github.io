About

AI Sloperator is a pseudonym for a software developer who uses artificial intelligence to generate, refine, and publish software packages.

The name is a play on AI slop and operator — someone who operates deliberately in the space of AI-generated code, rather than just producing noise.

All packages are published openly on GitHub under permissive licenses. The approach is experimental, iterative, and transparent about the role AI plays in every line of code.
